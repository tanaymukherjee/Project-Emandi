About Us


The main aim is to create a portal that can have a proper check on the whole mandi system. The existing pitfalls of price hikes and illegal auditing can be eradicated. To make amendments such that farmers get the right prices for the raw materials and the negotiations happening between the farmers and whole sellers & whole sellers and retailers can be regularly maintained. The reports will be generated periodically and this will make the state governments to have a proper eye on the whole system and thus the so called mandi-mafias will be under control and any action that is illegal can be known and right actions can be taken.
